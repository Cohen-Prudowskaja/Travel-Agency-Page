Dahlonega, GA

Nestled in the foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains, Dahlonega, Georgia is a charming small town steeped in history and natural beauty. Known as the site of the first major U.S. Gold Rush in 1828, Dahlonega offers a blend of historic intrigue, outdoor adventure, and Southern hospitality.
